Who Are We?

We are a group of high school coders from TISB looking to make a difference in the world.

If you are interested in knowing more , we will give you an option to re-direct. Otherwise we save on screen real estate.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a event in which people try to use tech in order to solve a real world problem. The goal is to create a relevant piece of hardware or software by the end which could potentially have a real world impact.

Why TISBHacks?

At TISBHacks, we expect the best of the best to show up. Come here if you want to test yourself against the pros or if you want to learn something new from your peers.
